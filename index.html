The next generation of online Go

Play Go against thousands of other players like never before. Typically, online Go, played on laptop screens or tablets, just doesn’t feel like a real Go game on a real wooden board. Digital Go Board fixes that by using the latest ePaper display technologies. Carefully crafted into a high quality wooden casing it gives you the best online Go experience ever possible.
Prepare your tea, turn on your concentration playlist, make yourself comfy and enjoy.

Syncs with your phone. And more.

The key idea is not to build yet another community of Go players. Instead, the goal is to extend the existing services out there and provide a much more relaxing atmosphere: Play e.g. online-go.com games on your physical Digital Go Board like any other match. Your opponent won’t even notice.
Customize your board, start matches, spin up testgames or puzzles - with your phone as the control the board can handle it all.
