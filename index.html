The next generation of online Go

Play Go against thousands of other players like never before. Until now, online Go matches don’t feel like matches on real wooden boards. Digital Go Board fixes that by using the latest ePaper display technologies in a way that it almost feels like playing on a traditional Go board. Carefully crafted into a high quality wooden casing it gives you the best online Go experience ever possible.
Prepare your tea, turn on your concentration playlist, make yourself comfy and enjoy.

Build upon existing services.

The key idea is not to build yet another community of Go players. Instead Digital Go Board extends the existing services out there: Play e.g. online-go.com games on your DGB like any other match. Your opponent won’t even notice.
Customize your board, start matches, spin up test-games or puzzles... with your phone as the control, the DGB can handle it all.
